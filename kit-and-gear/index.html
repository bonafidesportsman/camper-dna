Van life kit that's actually worth packing. Opinionated roundups and problem-solution guides for campervan accessories, cooking gear, outdoor kit and more.

Kit that earns its place

Every campervan has a finite amount of space. The gear that goes in it should earn that space — by being genuinely useful, by solving a real problem, or by being the right tool for a specific job rather than a compromised version of a better option you left at home.

The guides in this section are opinionated. We tell you what we actually use and why, what we tried and stopped using, and what we think is overhyped. Where we link to products, those are affiliate links — clearly labelled — that help fund the site.
