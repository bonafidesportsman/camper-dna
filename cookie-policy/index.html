This policy explains what cookies CamperDNA uses and why.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your preferences and understand how you use them.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies — Required for the site to function correctly. These include session management and security cookies. You cannot opt out of these without stopping use of the site.

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) — We use Google Analytics to understand which pages are most useful and how visitors move around the site. These cookies collect anonymised data only. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse new cookies, delete existing cookies, and browse in private mode. Deleting cookies may affect site functionality.
