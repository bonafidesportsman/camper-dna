The practical mechanics of buying

Once you've worked out what you want — van type, layout, systems — you still have to actually buy it. This section covers the practical process: finding the right vehicle, inspecting it properly, handling the paperwork, sorting insurance and understanding the real costs.

It's an area where people often rush. The excitement of finding a van can override the due diligence that protects a significant investment. The guides here are designed to slow that process down just enough to avoid the most common mistakes.
