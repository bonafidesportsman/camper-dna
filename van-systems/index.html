The systems that make a van

A campervan conversion is a collection of interdependent systems. The electrical system affects the heating options. The sleeping layout affects the storage options. The roof choice affects the sleeping layout. Understanding how these systems connect — before you spec a conversion or buy a pre-built van — is the difference between a van that works and one that compromises in unexpected places.

This section covers every major system in plain English. Not brand recommendations — that comes later, in the Kit & Gear section — but the underlying concepts you need to understand to make good decisions.
