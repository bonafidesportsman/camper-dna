CamperDNA started because buying a campervan is genuinely hard. Not hard like defusing a bomb, but hard in that quietly maddening way where you spend months on forums, Facebook groups, and YouTube rabbit holes, and still aren't sure if you're making the right call.

I've been through it. I spent the best part of a year trying to figure out the right setup before I bought my first van. Since then I've owned one for many years, customised it more times than I'd like to admit, and travelled in it across the UK and Europe — solo, with friends, and with my family and kids in tow, from weekends away to festival season.

All of that experience went into building this tool. It's meant to be a helpful shortcut, not a definitive authority. Think of it as getting advice from someone in the community who's been there — hopefully useful, honest, and offered in good faith, rather than a formal recommendation.
